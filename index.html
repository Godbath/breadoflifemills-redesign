Our Story

Bread of Life Mills was born from a desire to reconnect with real food. After years of homesteading and sustainable living, we discovered the power of freshly milled grain and traditional baking.

What began as a personal pursuit quickly became a calling—to bring integrity, nourishment, and craftsmanship back into everyday bread.

What We Offer

  • Fresh-milled flour blends
  • Artisan sourdough bread
  • Heritage and ancient grains
